Today's Pick: Casa Cuomo

Casa Cuomo is an Italian-Japanese fusion restaurant that makes very good food, especially if one wants to see pasta cooked on a cheese wheel. It has a very good ambience and it is good to visit for dinner.

Location: Senopati, Jalan Gunawarman no. 57 Kebayoran Baru, Jakarta Selatan.
